--- layout: home title: Eugene Wong subtitle: --- Hi! I'm Eugene 👋

I believe that data can help us go a long way with making better decisions. I'm passionate about evidence based decision making and how data can be used to help make ideas become reality. My areas of interest lies in the practical application of Welfare and Social Economics- the study of the relationship between resource allocation in society and social well-being.

The aim of this site is to harness the power of citizen science and to bring together key resources and ideas to help better our communities and society that we live in.

The key features of this site include:

  1. Publicy available databases relating to Social Work, Community Services & Social Policy
  2. My own data analysis and publications from publicly available databases

I welcome any feedback and any ideas on what else can be done to make this a more useful resource.
