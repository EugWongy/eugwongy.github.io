<summary>Information & Communications Technology</summary>

To my friends and family, I'm always the go to IT guy. Any issues with technology, find Eugene. So much so that "solving IT problems" is always my secondary duty at any job.

I was trained in Information and Communications Technology, specialising in wireless communication & networks, whilst serving as a signaller during National Service in the Singapore Armed Forces. During this time I completed part 1 of the Cisco Certified Networking Association (CCNA).

Despite this, my interest in computers started long ago as a kid. It all started with hacking passwords that my parents put on the computer to stop me from playing games. To be honest, it was the challenge that I liked. This led to putting computers together from spare parts when the computer was taken away. As a result, I'm very adept at self-directed learning to overcome technology challenges, as well as my problem-solving skills.

My professional experience includes investigating and gathering evidence relating to cybercrime, technology investigation, assisting victims of cybercrime, technical support with Information and Communication Technology, business technology advisory, ICT management and web administration.

I continue to provide technical support and website administration through our family business- [Itsy Websy Technology](https://itsywebsy.com.au).
